Liquid Foam

A whole surface of glass: drifting, merging bubbles that refract a living backdrop and tint it. Move your cursor — it carries a clear pocket through the foam.

← Back to the Liquid Glass collection

Your browser doesn’t support WebGL2, so the foam can’t render. 

import { createLiquidFoam, PRESETS } from "./src/liquid-foam.js";

const foam = createLiquidFoam({ canvas, config: PRESETS.Mercury });
foam.update({ coverage: 0.22 });   // live-tune any knob
